{{ _('Computing your optimal schedule') }}

{{ _('Sometimes, you may choose between multiple events such as CMs or TPs but only need to attend to one. The tool named compute tries all the possible combinations of picking one event of each type, every week, in order to find the 3 bests schedules which minimize the conflicts. If you want to be free during a given period, make sure to create a custom event to block this period of time. You can also remove some events before calling the compute function, to not take them into account. Because the compute function optimizes week per week, not all weeks must be the same. If you need to need to attend the same events every week, we encourage you to pick the week that suits your need from the results given by the compute tool and then manually select the events in your main schedule.') }} TODO: insert video

{{ _('Download an iCal file') }}

{{ _('One solution to export your schedule is to download it as an iCalendar file. Then, this file can easily be imported into your main calendar. The downside of this approach is that any further change in your schedule, from you or from ADE, won\'t be seen in your calendar. If you prefer to have calendar up-to-date, then choose the next option.') }}

{{ _('Subscription links') }}

{{ _('Maybe the best solution ADE Scheduler can offer is a subscription link. With this link, your calendar app will frequently download the latest events and you will never miss any change in your schedule ! Also, any manual change from your side, such as adding or removing a course, will be taken into account at the very next download of your schedule from you or from your app.') }}

{{ _('Account & Settings') }}

{{ _('What are the possibilities ?') }}

{{ _('On this website, no account is required to use most of the tools. Your session is stored during 30 days so you can easily leave this webpage, come back later and see that everything stays unchanged. Nonetheless, if 30 days is not enough or if you want to watch and modify your schedule on more than one device, we suggest you to create an account.
