Safe compute

{{ _('As you may know, computing a schedule from all possibilities is a very hard and complex problem. In fact, it is known to be a NP-Complete problem. As a result, some combinaison of courses can lead to a huge amount of time needed to computation. The Safe-compute option removes automatically before computation, all the possibilities for a same course that would lead to a conflict in the end. Even if some schedule possibilities are then not computed, the saved amount of time can be very important; and the final result should be the same as without the option.')}}
